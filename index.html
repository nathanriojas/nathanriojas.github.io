Biomechanics Experimental Laboratory (BEL): My research focused on the design and implementation of bioreactor systems to test the mechanical properties of the heart, specifically the mitral valve. This required extensive mechanical design and SolidWorks modeling to integrated load cells, actuators, and load cell interfaces into existing systems.

Rehabilitation with Insight from Robotics (Rewire) Laboratory: My research here focused on the design of a 12 bar, 1 degree of freedom gait rehabilitation robot to assist post-stroke and post-spinal cord injury victims.
