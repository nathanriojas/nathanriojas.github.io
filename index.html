About

Mechanical Engineer turned Software Developer

I have a background in mechanical engineering and robotics through the University of Texas at Austin where I received my bachelor's. While pursuing my degree, I became extremely interested in computer science after working in various labs that utilized software development to further their research.

That lead me to minor in computer science, and, after graduation, I set out to fully transition into the field of software engineering by enrolling in a computer science master's program at Georgia Tech. Artificial intelligence and machine learning have been two of the topics that I gravitate towards the most, and my most recent roles have been in the realm of data and data science engineering.

What other hobbies do I have?

I'm an avid martial arts enthusiast, and I train Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and Muay Thai regularly. I also love to travel, cook, and play guitar.
